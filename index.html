Email & Letter Templates

Use these templates to communicate professionally with builders, landlords, and trades.

📄 Builder Defect Claim Email

Hi [Builder Name], I am writing to formally notify you of a defect in the property at [Address]. Issue: [Describe the defect] Location: [Specify area] Date discovered: [Date] I have attached photos for reference. Please arrange inspection and repair within 7 days. Thank you, [Your Name] [Contact Number]

📋 90-Day Maintenance List

Hi [Builder Name], As per warranty requirements, I am providing a 90-day maintenance list. Items requiring attention: 1. [Item 1 with location] 2. [Item 2 with location] 3. [Item 3 with location] Photos attached. Please confirm receipt and advise repair timeline. Thank you, [Your Name]

🏠 Rental Repair Request

Hi [Landlord Name], I am writing to request urgent repairs to [Address]. Issue: [Describe repair needed] Date discovered: [Date] Photos: [Yes/No] This is affecting [explain impact]. Please confirm receipt and advise repair date. Thank you, [Tenant Name] [Contact Number]

🔧 Trades Person Quote Request

Hi [Trades Person Name], I am seeking a written quote for repair work at [Address]. Scope of work: [Describe clearly] Required materials: [List if applicable] Please provide: - Detailed quote (labour & materials) - Timeline for completion - Warranty information - Payment terms Thank you, [Your Name]

About PropertyMate AU

Our Mission

PropertyMate AU is designed to help Australian homeowners, first-home buyers, renters, landlords, and property investors understand property matters in simple, plain English.

What We Do

We provide general guidance on:

  • Builder defects and new home warranty claims

  • Investment property tax deductions and cashflow
  • Common property issues and solutions
  • Practical templates and calculators

When to Seek Professional Help

  • Legal: Warranty disputes, contract review → Consult a lawyer
  • Tax: Deductions, depreciation → Consult a tax accountant
  • Building: Structural issues, defect assessment → Consult a building inspector
  • Financial: Loan pre-approval, investment strategy → Consult a mortgage broker
  • Building Trades: Repairs and quotes → Get multiple quotes from licensed trades
